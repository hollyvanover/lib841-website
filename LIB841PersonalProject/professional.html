Professional

I have always wanted to be a librarian, especially after working in my local public library during my high school and college years. It sparked my love for books, and I knew that one day I wanted to become a librarian at a school. Even from a young age, I knew that I wanted to be on the same schedule as my kids so we could spend lots of time together! I have been lucky to be in the same school as my children for all of their schooling so far, and I am lucky enough to have my younget son in class next year!
